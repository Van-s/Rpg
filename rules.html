Gameplay Rules

Player 1 gets to choose their class first, then player 2 chooses their class and race, last player 2 chooses their race.

Lock your class and race at the bottom right of the player card when you are ready.

Each turn you get 2 rolls, either both into action points or one action point or one class roll.

Each turn you get 1 attack either standard attack or ability attack. Exceptions are the Warrior’s offensive stance and the Druid’s Wolf and Bear forms respectively. Furthermore, when you go into these stances and forms you get to attack that turn as well.

You can buy whenever you want but it takes an action point slot.

All roles are random, you can be extremely ‘lucky’ or ‘unlucky’ and get the same roles in a row.

Hover over your abilities to read their descriptions.

Your physical power is on the left side of the sword and your magical power on the right.
