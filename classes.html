Classes

All of the classes available to play

Archer

Sharp Shooter - Your next attack will be a critical strike, unless your opponent can no longer can be targeted. Critical strikes and multipliers can apply.

Snare - Reset a random di of your opppent.

Wind Em’ - Knock down your opponent, they lose a roll on their next turn.

Rapid Fire - Standard attack twice in one turn, critical strikes and multipliers can apply.

Honey Trap - Your opponent’s next ability will be delayed until their next turn, they may cast another move on that turn.

Fine Tuned - Add 1 permanent physical attack power to your base stats. First time use requires one action point, but every time used after that requires 2 action points. Cannot critically strike or multiply this ability, but other abilities or offensive attacks based off the additional physical power given by this ability can be critical strikes or multiplied.

Druid

Wolf Form - While in this form your physical attacks deal +2 damage.

Bear Form - While in this form gain 2 armor at the end of your turn

Regrow - Heal yourself and gain armor equal to half of your magical power. Critical strikes and multipliers can apply to the heal.

Enlightenment - Gain +2 magical power and +2 physical attack for your next two turns.

Suppression - Your oppenent loses 2 magical power and 2 phyical attack for one turn

MoonBlast - Land an beam that deals 1 and half times your magic power. Critical strikes and multipliers can apply.

Mage

Ice Blast - Double your magical power into one attack, it will land at the end of your opponent’s next turn. Spend Two action points to resolve at the end of your turn.

Cold Hearted - Melee opponents will fall if they try to attack you for the next two turns. They will then lose a roll on their next turn.

Ice Shards - Shoot one 1 missile each turn for 4 turns, dealing 1 damage each. Critical strikes and multipliers do not apply.

Arcane Focus - Your next offensive ability does double damage, including standard attacks. Cannot be used on top of another Arcane Focus, but may be used with other multipliers and critical strikes.

Free Will - Choose your next ability, only applies to current class.

Ice Queen - Temporarily increase your magical power by two for your next two turns. Critical strikes still apply but this ability cannot be multiplied by Arcane Focus.

Rogue

Quick Attack - Standard attack twice in one turn, critical strikes and multipliers can apply

Invisible - You can no longer be targeted by attacks, abilities already locked on you for the next turn are no longer targeted on you. Lasts two turns. Attacking brings you out of invisibility.

Bleed - Deal 1 damage to your target each turn for two turns. Applies on the turn used. Critical strikes and multipliers do not apply.

Sharpen - Your next offensive ability does double damage, including standard attacks. Cannot be used on top of another sharpen, but may be used with other multipliers and critical strikes.

Vengeance - You receive the damage that was dealt to you last turn again but in turn you get to add that same damage to your next attack. Critical strikes and multipliers can apply.

Steal - Pickpocket your opponent’s open class dice. If it is based off of magical power, use your current physical power to supplement the damage.

Shaman

Holy Fire - Cleanse your opponents with fire. Deal damage equal to your magic power for two turns automatically. Your allowed to cast another ability next turn. Critical strikes and multipliers can apply.

Mend - Heal yourself equal to your magical power times 1.5

Earth Shield - Block range attacks and abilities, halve damage from melee attacks and abilities.

Typhoon - Knock down your opponent, they lose a roll on their next turn.

Enhancement - Imbue your weapon with your magical power, land an attack which combines your attack and magic power.

Mother Totem - A random totem is placed until another ability is used.

Fire: will deal 1 damage at the end of your turn

Water: will heal for 1 at the end of your turn

Wind: will give you 1 magic power while active

Earth: will give you 1 attack power while active

Warrior

Blind Rage - Hit your opponent twice with two standard attacks, first attack will consume any multipliers or crits. Only use one action point.

Overwhelm - Daze your opponent, they can no longer attack or use an ability for the next turn.

Concuss - Reset your opponent’s multiplier.

Offensive Stance - Deal an extra 1 damage on all standard attacks while also receiving an extra one damage.

Parry - Prepare your sword to deflect your opponent’s next attack. Take no damage and deal half the damage that was coming to you (rounded up) back at your opponent automatically.

God’s Favorite - Gain 6 temporary armor on your next turn lose 2 armor.
